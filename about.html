About Me

Data Scientist, Chemical Engineer, Tech Enthusiast

Passionate about the intersection of technology and creativity, I am a dedicated individual with a love for coding, robotics, music, and photography. As a programmer, I find joy in turning ideas into functional and innovative solutions, exploring the limitless possibilities of computing.
